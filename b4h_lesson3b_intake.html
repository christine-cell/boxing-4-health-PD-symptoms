01 ‚Äî Purpose of the Intake Form

The prospective client intake form is completed before the formal assessment. It gives you essential background on the client's diagnosis, mobility, fall history, and current activity level ‚Äî so you arrive at the assessment already informed, not starting from zero.

Think of this as your pre-assessment snapshot. It helps you determine which assessment tests are appropriate, what red flags to watch for, and which class might be the right fit ‚Äî before you ever see them move.
