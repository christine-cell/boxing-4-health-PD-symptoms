01 â€” What Is PD Warrior?

Born in Australia. Built on Science.

PD Warrior is an exercise-based Parkinson's treatment program founded in 2011 by two specialist neurological physiotherapists in Australia. It was built from the ground up on the principles of neuroplasticity â€” specifically designed to retrain the brain and improve Parkinson's symptoms through high-intensity, neuroactive exercise.

The program's founder, Melissa McConaghy, is a specialist neurological physiotherapist with a Master's in Health Science in Neurological Physiotherapy. She was awarded Australian Physiotherapist of the Year in 2019 and has spent over 20 years working with people with Parkinson's. PD Warrior has since grown into the world's largest online Parkinson's exercise community.

A published pilot study by Tucak et al. (2024, Internal Medicine Journal) provided the first peer-reviewed evidence that the PD Warrior 10-Week Challenge program significantly improves motor outcomes, mobility, daily living, and quality of life in people with early Parkinson's.
